About XD Magazine

At XD, we aim to connect, entertain, and engage with our audience through rich Filipino narratives and personal stories. Our mission is to create a space for everyone to enjoy and be inspired by true Filipino experiences.

We focus on high-quality content that explores Filipino culture, history, and stories that shape the modern world. Our content includes featured articles, comedy, and narratives that resonate with our audience.

Explore Our Subtopics

Subtopic 1: Filipino Stories

XD celebrates Filipino stories that span across history and culture. These stories highlight our rich heritage, bringing a deeper understanding of the Filipino way of life.

With a growing audience for gaming and esports, XD dives deep into this industry, showcasing Filipino talent and the rise of local gaming communities.

Technology is rapidly shaping our future, and XD covers the latest innovations, from cutting-edge gadgets to breakthroughs in various fields that affect Filipino lives.
